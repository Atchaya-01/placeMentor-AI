Scope Document – Placementor AI
Project Title

Placementor AI – AI-Based Placement Prediction and Career Guidance System

1. Introduction

Placementor AI is a web-based intelligent placement assistance system designed to help students analyze their placement readiness and receive career guidance based on their academic and skill-related data.

The system collects student details through an interactive web form, stores the data in a structured database, and provides visual insights using dashboards. Additionally, AI-based recommendations are integrated to guide students toward suitable career paths and improvements.

The project aims to bridge the gap between student performance and placement readiness by providing meaningful predictions and analytics.

2. Purpose of the Project

The primary purpose of Placementor AI is to:

Predict placement readiness of students
Analyze academic and skill performance
Provide AI-based suggestions
Display placement insights using dashboards
Help students improve their placement chances
Assist institutions in tracking placement trends

This system enables data-driven decision-making for both students and placement coordinators.
